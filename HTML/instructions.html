Instructions

When you click select map you will be preseneted with 3 options for maps and when you select one you can choose a difficulty. Once you have picked you will see on the left of the screen a display and 3 towers to choose from.

If you click a tower it will and you have enough money you can place it, it will follow your cursor and will be placed when you click again. Once the tower is placed you can click on the tower to access the upgrades. There are 3 types of tower: a normal one, and long rnaged one and one that shoots in 4 directions

Throughout the game you will come across 4 different types of enemies, The red enemies have 1 health, the blue ones have 2 health, green have 3 and purple have 4. When an enemy is shot it will take damage and change colour
